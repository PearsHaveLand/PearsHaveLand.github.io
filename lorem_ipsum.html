Practice Project: Progress Bar

I wrote this project up to practice creating a progress bar. For this specific page, the progress bar reflects how far you've scrolled to the bottom of the page. I saw this on some news site, and thought it might be fun to recreate. Personally, I can't stand these types of things. But I'm not a big fan of any sticky elements on my browser screen. So you won't find this on anything I actually host for my own personal use, except for this specific page.

The following is just a bunch of placeholder "Lorem Ipsum" text, to let you scroll through the page and play with the progress bar.
